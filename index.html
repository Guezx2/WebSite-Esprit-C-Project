How To Make a Reservation

Choose Your Parking Spot

Log in to the Parky app and browse the list of available parking locations. Use filters to select the area, parking type, or services you need. Once you find the perfect spot, click on it to proceed.

You can view detailed descriptions of each location, including available amenities and reviews from other users. Our interactive map ensures you find the nearest and most convenient parking in seconds.

Set Your Reservation Details

Select the date, time, and duration of your reservation. Choose any additional services, like valet or EV charging, if needed. Parky makes it easy to customize your booking to fit your schedule.

Don’t forget to specify your vehicle type and size for accurate pricing. The app’s intuitive interface lets you adjust your preferences with just a few taps.

Confirm and Park Stress-Free

Review your reservation details, check the price, and confirm your booking. You’ll receive an instant confirmation with all the details. Arrive at your selected parking location, and your spot will be ready and waiting for you!

Plus, you can easily modify or cancel your booking if your plans change. With Parky, you’re always in control of your parking experience.
